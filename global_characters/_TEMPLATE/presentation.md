## Psychology

> This is the core the agent needs to write them truthfully.
> Be honest — especially about the unflattering parts.

**Core driver:**
*(What do they fundamentally want? Not their stated goal — the deeper need underneath it.)*

**Core fear:**
*(What are they most afraid of? Not surface fears — the existential one.)*

**Self-image:**
*(How do they see themselves? Is it accurate?)*

**The gap:**
*(Where does their self-image diverge from reality? What do they not see about themselves?)*

**Default coping mechanism:**
*(What do they do when things get hard? Work more? Withdraw? Control others?
Joke? Go quiet?)*

**What they're proud of:**
*(Can be a strength or a flaw they've convinced themselves is a strength)*

**What they're ashamed of:**
*(The thing they would least want a stranger to know)*

**What they avoid saying:**
*(Specific words, phrases, or topics they deflect from)*
